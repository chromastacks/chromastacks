WRAPPINGS (blue monochromes)

This series emerged during the height of COVID. I created paintings of varying sizes with pockets that I inserted into one another. Creating a space for another became the only possible act. We were all confined to our homes, contained by something greater than ourselves.

Wrapping is a customizable, on-demand digital painting system. Once selected, I will paint it according to the selection offering chart.
