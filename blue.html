WRAPPINGS (blue monochromes)

This series emerged during the height of COVID[cite: 5, 7]. I created paintings of varying sizes with pockets that I inserted into one another. Creating a space for another became the only possible act[cite: 9]. We were all confined to our homes, contained by something greater than ourselves[cite: 10].

Wrapping is a customizable, on-demand digital painting system. Once selected, I will paint it according to the selection offering chart.
