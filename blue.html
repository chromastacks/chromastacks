WRAPPINGS (Blue monochromes)

This series emerged at the height of COVID. I created paintings of varying sizes with pockets that inserted into one another. Creating a space for another became the only possible act. We were all confined to our homes, contained by something greater than ourselves.

Wrapping is a customizable, on-demand digital painting system.
